Galileo Ways of Work

A socio technical continuous flow model where Discovery, Delivery, and Operations move in parallel, share information continuously, and adapt in real time to uncertainty.

music_note Executive metaphor – dance, not phases

Working at Galileo is closer to dancing with the customer problem than following a rigid choreography. We do not work in a sequence like first listen, then learn the rhythm, then try to dance. Discovery, Delivery, and Operations must hear the music at the same time, react to each other, and adjust in short loops. That is how we reduce residual uncertainty and build resilient flows for e commerce, stores, lab and logistics in Mexico, Chile, and Colombia.

explore Discovery

Clarify problem and context

Discovery reduces uncertainty enough so that a decision is possible. It does not try to know everything. It clarifies the problem, constraints, and impacts with real context from stores, lab and customers.

  • • Archetypes, personas, Jobs to Be Done, context of use
  • • Explicit assumptions and hypotheses
  • • Problem framing, opportunity and impact
  • • Domain modeling: EventStorming, Domain Storytelling
  • • Early feasibility and viability checks
  • • Draft SLIs and SLOs for what success means

Feeds Delivery with clarified scope and constraints. Receives operational reality from Operations to refine assumptions.

code Delivery

Build operable solutions

Delivery creates solutions that are ready to live in production. It balances speed, safety, and technical coherence for Odoo, PoS, lab, logistics and data.

  • • Delivery modes: greenfield, brownfield, strangler, firefighting
  • • Threat modeling, spikes, PoCs
  • • Sacrificial architecture when learning is the main goal
  • • Architecture coherence and Well Architected principles
  • • Fitness functions and living documentation
  • • Observability instrumentation and quality gates

Works with Discovery to adjust feasibility and constraints. Works with Operations to protect error budgets and resilience.

monitor_heart Operations

Prove value in reality

Operations is where the system meets customers, stores and lab. It reveals what really happens: value, bottlenecks, and fragility.

  • • Error budgets, SLI and SLO tracking
  • • Incident detection, triage and resolution
  • • User behavior and operational load tracking
  • • A B testing and impact measurement
  • • Data quality, contracts and lifecycle
  • • Resilience testing and failure analysis

Feeds Delivery with new insights and constraints. Feeds Operations with performance, reliability and regression patterns.
