Customer Value Stream Journey

End-to-end flow from the perspective of 4ojos (customer) and casimirx (store and lab operators). This journey is the central driver of our work. It is not a linear process, but a continuous flow of value creation and feedback. Discovery clarifies the path, Delivery builds the means of travel, and Operations ensures the journey is smooth and reliable. The three tracks synchronize constantly to adapt to the customer's real-world needs and context.
