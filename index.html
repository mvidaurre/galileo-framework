exploreDiscovery

Clarify problem and context

Discovery reduces uncertainty enough so that a decision is possible. It clarifies the problem, constraints, and impacts with real context from stores, lab, and customers.

  • • Archetypes, personas, JTBD, context of use
  • • Explicit assumptions and hypotheses
  • • Problem framing, opportunity and impact
  • • Domain modeling and early feasibility checks
  • • Draft SLIs/SLOs for success definition

Feeds Delivery with clarified scope. Receives operational reality from Operations to refine assumptions.

codeDelivery

Build operable solutions

Delivery creates solutions ready to live in production. It balances speed, safety, and coherence across Odoo, PoS, lab, logistics, and data.

  • • Delivery modes: greenfield, brownfield, strangler, firefighting
  • • Threat modeling, spikes, PoCs, sacrificial architecture
  • • Fitness functions, observability, quality gates
  • • Architecture coherence and paved roads

Works with Discovery to adjust feasibility and constraints. Works with Operations to protect error budgets and resilience.

monitor_heartOperations

Prove value in reality

Operations is where the system meets customers, stores, and lab. It reveals what really happens: value, bottlenecks, and fragility.

  • • Error budgets, SLI and SLO tracking
  • • Incident detection, triage, and resolution
  • • User behavior and operational load tracking
  • • A/B testing and impact measurement
  • • Resilience testing and failure analysis

Feeds Discovery with new insights and constraints. Feeds Delivery with performance, reliability, and regression patterns.
